## Global Style Rules (apply to ALL images)

**Tell Nano Banana this before generating:**
> All images must follow this consistent style:
> - Dark navy background (hex #0D0D1F or very dark blue-black)
> - Clean flat vector/illustration style (NOT photorealistic)
> - Minimal, modern, educational
> - No readable real text (use squiggly lines or abstract shapes for text)
> - 16:9 aspect ratio (landscape)
> - High resolution (minimum 1200px wide)
